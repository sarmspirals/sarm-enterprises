Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy? +
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused products in original packaging. Return shipping is free for defective items. For change of mind returns, a restocking fee may apply.
How long does shipping take? +
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for delivery within 2-3 business days. International shipping times vary by destination.
Do you offer warranty on products? +
Yes, most products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty. Extended warranty options are available for purchase on select items.
Can I track my order? +
Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.
Do you offer bulk discounts? +
Yes, we offer volume discounts for business customers. Please contact our sales team at sales@sarmenterprises.com for custom quotes.
